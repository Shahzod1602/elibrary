About Us

AUT E-Library — center for knowledge, science and modern educational resources

About the E-Library

AUT E-Library is the central knowledge hub of our university, with a physical collection of 603 books and 37 eBooks. Our E-Library provides comprehensive educational resources for students, teachers and researchers.

To borrow books, you must be a library member. Each member can borrow up to 3 books at a time. Books are issued for 14 days, with a one-time 7-day extension available.

Library Rules

  • A student ID or staff ID card is required for library registration.
  • Books are issued for 14 days. The period can be extended once for 7 days.
  • A fine of 1,000 UZS is charged for each overdue day.
  • Personal bags are not allowed in the library — lockers are available.
  • Eating and loud talking are prohibited in the reading hall.
  • Lost or damaged books must be compensated at full value.

Help

Our librarians will help you find the right book, use digital resources and search for sources for scientific research. You can also get help via Telegram bot: @AI_librarianbot

Book Club

Every 2 weeks a book is selected and discussed among group members. The book club is a great opportunity for sharing knowledge, discovering new ideas and developing a reading culture.

To register, visit the library or write via Telegram bot.
